Description

PHOGS! takes you and a friend on a vibrant, cartoony adventure as a double-ended dog. Stretch your co-op abilities as you learn to work together, sharing a controller and a body in a bizarre and puzzling world!

History

PHOGS! is the first game we are developing as Bit Loom. Currently being developed for PC and Consoles at Biome Collective in Dundee, it aims to be a short experience filled with strange puzzles, creatures and interactions. It has been showcased at various events including Insomnia61, EGX 2017 and IndieCade 2017.

Features

  • Double-ended dogs!
  • Vibrant, cartoony worlds to explore!
  • Share a controller with your friend.
  • Meet strange creatures and solve bizarre puzzles!
  • Simple controls with surprising interactions.
