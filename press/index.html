Description

We are three friends who love making small, fun and experimental games. We are also a part of Biome Collective where we work and play.

History

Background

We formed Bit Loom after several years of collaboration at Abertay University on game jams as well as individual work on various projects including Everything is Peachy and BAFTA award winning Among the Stones. Our first project as a studio is PHOGS! a double-ended dog adventure.
