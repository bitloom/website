Go on a double-ended dog adventure with your friend!
PHOGS! takes you and a friend on a double-ended physics dog adventure in a vibrant and colourful world filled with puzzles to solve and strange creatures to help. Cooperate and coordinate as you each use half of a controller to control one end of the dog, stretching and grabbing to grow jumpkins, herd munchlets and feed the hungry world worms. PHOGS! is a game with simple controls and a silly atmosphere that anyone can play with family and friends.
